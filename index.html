Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the beginning at the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number of points to your score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes over the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
